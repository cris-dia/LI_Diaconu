Stand With Ukraine!

We're raising awarness about the ongoing war in Ukraine, and 50% of all our profits will be directed to support the war refugees. The ongoing conflict in Ukraine has caused immense suffering for the people of Ukraine, and it is a humanitarian crisis that deserves our attention and concern. Civilians continue to be killed, injured, and displaced, and the situation has only worsened in recent years. It is important that we raise awareness about the war in Ukraine and support efforts to bring peace and stability to the region. We must also remember the human cost of war and work towards a world where conflicts are resolved through peaceful means. Stand with Ukraine, stand with mr. Zelenski, stand for peace!
